About Super U!

Super U! was created in response to the rising numbers of youth suicide.

Partnered with the Suicide Prevention Lifeline, Super U! is a companion app made for youth.

Super U! offers mental health resources, activities, a mood chart and a safety plan for youth to take control of their mental health status.
